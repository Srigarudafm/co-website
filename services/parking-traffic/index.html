Parking & Traffic

Parking lots are vulnerable to all sorts of crimes and illegal activity. It is not uncommon to hear stories of employees being robbed when they leave the office late at night, or vehicles being broken into while residents sleep. When people are on your property, you want them to feel safe and secure. Not only is this good for the reputation of your business, it’s good business all around. 

Security doesn’t start once you enter the front door of your business. Parking lots are hotbeds of criminal activity, including theft, assault and vandalism. In fact, studies indicate that 90% property crimes occur in parking lots, parking garages or other parking structures. These statistics should be alarming to those who own or operate any kind of property that has parking facilities. 

If you are responsible for a commercial building, residential community, shopping center or other property that has parking areas, you understand that security vulnerabilities in your parking lot can put people at risk and be a liability for your business. Protect your business by hiring professional security guards from SGFM.
